SA Harvest implements massive food rescue initiative in Cape Town and Johannesburg preceding the Nation’s 21-day lockdown

SA Harvest, a food rescue organisation that has already delivered 40 000 meals to the homeless in Cape Town since its launch in November 2019, took to the roads yesterday to rescue pre-expired food from airline catering companies, restaurants, bakeries and delis who closed their doors and kitchens at midnight last night. The rescued food will be distributed to vulnerable people in Cape Town and Johannesburg, during the country’s lockdown....

SA Harvest launches in South Africa

A revolutionary food rescue organisation committed to nourishing our nation

SA Harvest, an NPC committed to food rescue in South Africa, hosted their official launch at The Service Dining Rooms, Cape Town, on Wednesday 16 October, aptly, on World Food Day. The organisation’s purpose is to eliminate hunger and food waste through education, and by the rescuing and re-distribution of quality surplus food...
