GIVE A LITTLE LOVE



DONATE FOOD

We rescue food from a wide range of sources including fruit and vegetable markets, supermarkets, hotels, wholesalers, farmers, stadiums, corporate events, corporate kitchens, catering companies, shopping centres, delis, cafes, restaurants, film and TV shoots, boardrooms and more.

If you are any of the above and want to donate food to SA HARVEST please contact OUR TEAM.



DONATE MONEY

There are several ways in which you can donate money to SA Harvest

If you would like to donate money to help feed the 13 million South Africans that experience hunger every day, please contact OUR TEAM for further details.
